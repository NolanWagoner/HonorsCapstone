Questionnaire

Answer the questions below to be paired up with advice specific to your cyber security needs.

1) Do you use the same 1-3 passwords for most or all of your online accounts? (includes similar passwords ex: change of only a few characters)



2) Do you have antivirus software on your computer?



3) Do you avoid updating software or put updates off until they are required?



4) Do you click on suspicious links or links that lead to suspicious websites?



5) Do you leave unlocked devices unattended?




6) Do you use unsecured public Wi-Fi
